Design a mobile app called "Suzi Flies" — a personal flight morning routine planner for a frequent business traveler. The app should fit entirely within one mobile screen (no scrolling), with an elegant, intuitive, and minimal interaction design inspired by Monet's Water Lilies — using a palette of Dark Green (#2D4A3E), Moss Green (#6B7C5C), Beige (#E8DCC8), Rosy Brown (#C4967A), and Midnight Green (#1A3A3A), with soft watercolor-like gradients and subtle texture overlays to evoke an impressionist painting feel.

App Structure & Features:
1. Header — "Suzi Flies"
Top of screen. App name in elegant serif font. Below it, a single text input field labeled "Flight No." (e.g. MU5101) — this is the only field the user needs to fill in each time. Once entered, the app auto-calculates all time nodes and displays them instantly.
2. Alarm Hero Block — "Wake Bloom"
A large, visually dominant card in the center-upper area. Displays the calculated alarm time in large typography (e.g. 05:30). Subtitle: "Set your alarm to this time." This is the most important information — make it feel like the centerpiece of a painting, surrounded by soft color gradients.
3. Timeline — "The Journey"
A compact, elegant vertical timeline below the hero block showing 6 key nodes, each with:

An icon + label (keep labels short and charming)
The calculated clock time
A small editable field or tap-to-input for actual time spent (to compare with default)
A subtle diff indicator (e.g. "+5 min" in rosy brown, or "on time" in moss green)

The 6 nodes and their default durations (pre-set, editable in Settings):

🌸 Wake Bloom — Alarm rings (calculated start)
🛏️ Linger — Snooze/lazy time: 20 min
🪥 Polish — Wash up & pack: 30 min
🚗 Roll Out — Travel to airport: 30 min (fixed: Shanghai Hongqiao Airport)
🛃 Clear — Security check: 20 min
🚪 Gate — Arrive at gate (15 min before boarding)
✈️ Board — Boarding begins: 20 min before departure

The timeline should feel like lily pads floating on water — soft connectors between nodes, not rigid lines.
4. Settings Drawer — "My Ritual"
A collapsible bottom section or a small gear icon that opens an inline panel (not a new screen). Shows all default time values as editable fields. Label: "My Ritual — your defaults, your pace." Changes are saved as user preferences.
5. Visual & Interaction Details:

Background: deep midnight green with soft watercolor wash effect
Cards: semi-transparent beige/moss green with frosted glass feel
Typography: mix of elegant serif (for times/hero) and clean sans-serif (for labels)
Micro-interactions: gentle fade-in when flight number is entered; timeline nodes appear with a soft bloom animation
Color coding: default = moss green, delayed = rosy brown, early = dark green
All content must be visible within one iPhone screen (375×812px) without scrolling
No unnecessary buttons or screens — everything lives on one canvas
